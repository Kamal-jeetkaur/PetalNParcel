Frequently Asked Questions

What is Petal N Parcel?

Petal N Parcel is your premier online destination for exquisite bouquets, delightful cakes, lush plants, and thoughtful gifts for every occasion. We aim to make gifting easy and memorable.

How can I track my order?

You can track your order using the "Track Order" button in the navigation bar. Simply enter your order details, and we'll provide real-time updates on your delivery.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes, we offer same-day delivery for select products and locations. Please check the product page or enter your delivery pin code to see if this option is available for your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods, including major credit/debit cards, net banking, and popular UPI services for your convenience.
